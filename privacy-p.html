Privacy Policy for Telescope Ajloun Tourist Guide Application

Last updated: 26/10/2024

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use our application, Ajloun Tourist Guide ("the Application"). Our goal is to help you discover beautiful tourist areas in Ajloun and allow you to contribute content by uploading images. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your information responsibly.

We collect the following types of information:

5. Your Choices

You can control the following privacy settings:

Our Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13.
